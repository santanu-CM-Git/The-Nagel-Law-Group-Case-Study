# The Nagel Law Group — Website Case Study

## About Project

The Nagel Law Group is a California-based family law firm focused on divorce, child custody, spousal support, mediation, and post-judgment modifications.

The firm required a modern, trustworthy, and conversion-focused digital presence that reflected Attorney Jennifer Nagel’s extensive legal experience while simplifying the process for potential clients seeking legal guidance during emotionally challenging situations.

---

# The Client

The Nagel Law Group is a family law practice serving clients across California with personalized legal representation in divorce and custody matters.

Led by Jennifer Nagel, a seasoned attorney with more than 25 years of experience, the firm emphasizes compassionate guidance, strategic legal support, and clear communication throughout the legal process.

### Client Objectives

The website needed to:

* Establish credibility and authority in family law
* Generate qualified consultation requests
* Simplify complex legal information for users
* Improve mobile responsiveness and accessibility
* Create a calming and professional user experience
* Showcase attorney expertise and client testimonials
* Improve local visibility for California family law searches

---

# Problems

## 1. Outdated User Experience & Information Structure

The previous website lacked a clear information hierarchy and intuitive navigation. Visitors struggled to quickly understand legal services, consultation processes, and available support options, reducing engagement and conversions.

## 2. Weak Trust & Credibility Signals

Although the firm had extensive legal experience and a strong client history, the website did not effectively communicate expertise, reducing visitor confidence during decision-making.

## 3. Complex Legal Information Presentation

Family law topics—including custody, alimony, and property division—can feel overwhelming. The content required restructuring to become more approachable and easier to understand.

## 4. Mobile Responsiveness & Accessibility

The website required optimization across mobile and tablet devices to ensure a seamless browsing experience for users accessing legal services on multiple screen sizes.

## 5. SEO & Performance Optimization

The existing website lacked strong technical SEO foundations, limiting search visibility and reducing overall performance and speed.

# The Solution

The project focused on designing and developing a professional, clean, and conversion-oriented law firm website that balanced legal authority with a compassionate user experience.

## Strategic UI/UX Redesign

The website experience was completely redesigned with a structured layout, calming visual hierarchy, and strategically positioned consultation CTAs to increase engagement and lead generation.

### Key Improvements

* Simplified navigation architecture
* Clear service categorization
* Improved readability and spacing
* Mobile-first responsive layouts
* Conversion-focused call-to-actions
* Streamlined consultation flow
